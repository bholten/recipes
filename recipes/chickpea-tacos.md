# chickpea tacos
--
## Ingredients
spiced chickpeas:
- 1 can chickpeas
- 2 tsp evoo
- 3/4 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp oregano
- 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- cayenne pepper
- lime juice

roast the chickpeas in a cast iron pan, toss with spices

cilantro pesto:
- 1/2 cup roasted cashews
- 2 1/2 cups cilantro
- 2 cloves garlic
- 1 jalepeno pepper
- 1 lemon or lime
- 1/4 tsp salt
- black pepper
- 1/3 cup evoo

blend (like a pesto)

serve together as tacos
