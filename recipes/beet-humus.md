# beet humus
--

## Ingredients
- 1 beet
- 1 can chickpeas
- 1/4 tsp baking soda
- 1/3 cup tahini
- 1 lemon
- 3 cloves garlic
- 1/4 tps cumin
- 1/4 tsp coriander
- 1 tsp salt
- black pepper
- 1/2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil

## Instructions
roast the beats in 425 f for 45 to 60 mins

rinse and simmer chickpeas

drain and rinse chickpeas

blend humus: beets, chickpeas, tahini, lemon zest, lemon juice, garlic, cumin, etc.
